# Template Anchor: General Inquiry

## Purpose
Respond to general questions about your business -- hours, services, pricing,
location, or "tell me more" emails. These are people at the top of the funnel
who need helpful information delivered warmly.

## Tone
Helpful and welcoming. Answer their question directly, then give them an easy
next step. Don't overwhelm with information they didn't ask for.

## Required Information to Address
- Whatever specific question they asked
- Basic business details (hours, location, contact) if relevant
- A clear next step (visit website, call, come in, etc.)

## Must Include
- Direct answer to their question
- Business contact information when relevant
- A friendly invitation to reach out with more questions

## Sign-Off
Casual, first-name only:
> -- [Owner First Name]

## Never
- Sound like a FAQ page or automated response
- Ignore or sidestep their actual question
- Overwhelm with information they didn't request
- Use corporate language ("per your inquiry", "we appreciate your interest")

## Guardrails
- If they ask about pricing, provide only verified/published prices
- If they ask something you can't answer, flag for human review
- Keep it to 4-6 sentences unless more detail is genuinely needed

## Example Flow
1. Warm greeting
2. Direct answer to their question
3. One or two helpful details they might also want
4. Friendly close with invitation to ask more
